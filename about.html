Program Overview

This site presents the role and contributions of youth to national defense and security. From military training programs to volunteer actions, Vietnamese youth are active, creative, and ready to serve the nation.

"Where youth are needed, youth will be there; where something is difficult, youth step up" – this motto continues to inspire young people across many fields, especially in national defense.

Participation Statistics

Activity Number of participants
Annual military service ~100,000 (per year)
"Military Semester" training program 10,000+ (2025)
Volunteer rapid-response units 5,000 (nationwide)

Goals & Directions

  • Raise national defense awareness: Help youth understand patriotic traditions and be ready to defend the country.
  • Skills training: Organize military-style training, survival skills, and rescue training for young people.
  • Foster innovation: Encourage youth to research and apply technology in defense (robots, drones, etc.).
