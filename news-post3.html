Military Semester 2025 opens

15/09/2025

The Military Semester 2025 program officially launched today, marking a significant milestone in youth military education. The program has attracted unprecedented interest, with over 5,000 students from various universities and colleges across the country registered to participate.

This semester's program includes:

  • Basic military training and physical conditioning
  • Leadership and teamwork development
  • National defense education
  • Emergency response and survival skills
  • Discipline and character building exercises

Major General Le Van Hung, program director, outlined the expanded curriculum: "This year's program incorporates modern training methodologies while maintaining our core focus on discipline, patriotism, and practical skills development."

Participating students will undergo training at various military facilities across the country, guided by experienced instructors from both military and academic backgrounds. The program spans 12 weeks and includes both theoretical and practical components.

New additions to this year's curriculum include:

  • Advanced technology in national defense
  • Cyber security awareness
  • Environmental protection in military operations
  • International humanitarian law

The program has received strong support from educational institutions and has become a valuable addition to students' academic experience, promoting both personal growth and national service awareness.
