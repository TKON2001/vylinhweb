Youth police units mobilize to protect public order

25/09/2025

Provincial youth police units have launched a major campaign to enhance public safety and community protection. The initiative has successfully mobilized hundreds of young volunteers who are working alongside local law enforcement to maintain neighborhood security.

Key aspects of the campaign include:

  • Regular neighborhood patrols during peak hours
  • Community outreach and education programs
  • Traffic safety monitoring near schools
  • Anti-theft awareness campaigns
  • Emergency response coordination

Colonel Tran Duc Anh, head of the Provincial Police Department, praised the youth involvement: "The enthusiasm and dedication of our young volunteers have significantly improved our community policing efforts. Their presence has not only enhanced security but also strengthened the bond between law enforcement and citizens."

The program has already shown promising results, with a 30% decrease in petty crimes in participating neighborhoods. Local residents have expressed strong support for the initiative, noting improved sense of security in their communities.

Looking ahead, organizers plan to expand the program to more districts and introduce additional training modules for volunteers.
