Volunteer campaign supports border communities

05/09/2025

A comprehensive volunteer campaign to support border communities has successfully concluded its first phase, making a significant impact on local development and education. The initiative brought together youth organizations and local authorities to address various needs in border regions.

Key achievements of the campaign:

  • Distribution of educational supplies to over 500 disadvantaged students
  • Organization of legal awareness workshops for local residents
  • Implementation of basic healthcare programs
  • Cultural exchange activities with border communities
  • Infrastructure improvement projects

Ms. Pham Thu Hang, campaign coordinator, shared her insights: "Our volunteers have done more than just distribute supplies. They've created lasting connections with these communities and gained deep understanding of border region challenges."

The campaign focused on several vital areas:

Educational Support

Beyond providing school supplies, volunteers conducted tutoring sessions and educational workshops for local students. Special attention was given to maintaining educational continuity in remote areas.

Legal Awareness

Regular information sessions helped residents understand their rights and responsibilities, particularly regarding border-related regulations and available government support programs.

Community Development

Volunteers worked alongside local authorities to implement small-scale infrastructure projects and organize community events that strengthen social bonds.

The success of this initiative has led to plans for expanding the program to other border regions in the coming months.
